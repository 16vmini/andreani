# Andreani Group App — Sales Pitch

## The Scale

This isn't a local business. Andreani Group is an international suspension empire:
- **Founded 1987** by ex-World Motocross champion Giuseppe Andreani
- **Exclusive Öhlins distributor** for Italy + Eastern Europe (16 countries)
- **Andreani USA Inc** — North American branch since 2023
- **Racing service** at World Superbike, Motocross, Enduro championships
- **Training academy** — courses that certify authorised centres
- **Professional tools division** — DB4/DS1 dynos, vacuum pumps, specialist equipment
- **Distributed brands** — Mivv, Samco, PFC, BCA
- **Departments:** Motorcycle road, motorcycle off-road, MTB, cars
- **30+ staff** across technical, commercial, and support roles

This is a company that operates like a franchise. Authorised centres worldwide, racing teams, course attendees, and thousands of end customers. They need a digital platform to connect it all.

## The Problem

Right now:
- **Service reminders don't exist** — customers forget until their suspension fails
- **Dyno data stays in the workshop** — printed or emailed, never revisited
- **Suspension setup is tribal knowledge** — locked in technicians' heads
- **Course booking** is probably manual — email/phone
- **Finding an authorised centre** requires searching a website list
- **No digital service history** — paper stamps, easily lost
- **Racing service booking** is ad-hoc
- **Product catalogue** is scattered across multiple sub-websites (andreanitools.com, attrezzature.andreanigroup.com, etc.)

## The Solution: The Andreani App

One platform for the entire Andreani ecosystem. Customer-facing AND B2B.

## Key Features

### 1. My Garage — The Suspension Dashboard 🏠
Every rider/driver registers their vehicles and suspension components. The app knows:
- What suspension you have (Öhlins NIX30, TTX36, Andreani cartridge kit...)
- When it was last serviced
- What setup you're running
- When the next service is due

**Push notifications:** "Your Öhlins fork service is due in 500km — book at TW Suspension Tech (nearest centre, 12 miles away)."

**This alone drives recurring service revenue through the entire authorised network.**

### 2. Digital Dyno Reports 📊
Andreani makes the DB4 and DS1 dynos. Their authorised centres use them daily. Currently that data stays on a laptop in the workshop.

**With the app:** After a dyno test, results upload to the customer's account. Interactive graphs: force vs velocity, compression and rebound curves, before/after comparison. The customer can:
- Review their results anytime
- Compare to factory baseline
- Share on social media ("Just had my Öhlins dyno'd at Andreani 🏍️📊")
- Track changes over time (new springs, valve shims, oil viscosity)

**Shared dyno reports = viral marketing.** Every shared graph has the Andreani logo on it.

### 3. Setup Wizard — 35 Years of Racing Data 🔧
Giuseppe Andreani has been setting up suspension at world championship level since the 1990s. That knowledge is priceless but it's locked in people's heads.

**The Setup Wizard** digitises it:
- Select your bike/car
- Input rider weight, riding style, experience, track/road use
- App recommends: preload, compression (high/low speed), rebound settings
- Based on data from thousands of setups across decades of racing

**This positions Andreani as THE suspension authority.** Every rider in the world wants to know "what should my settings be?" Now there's an app for that — with Andreani's name on it.

### 4. Course Booking 📚
Andreani runs technical courses that certify mechanics as authorised centres. Currently booked manually.

**App enables:**
- Browse courses (motorcycle, car, MTB, specific topics)
- See dates, locations, availability
- Book and pay in-app
- Receive course materials digitally
- Certification tracked in-app (proof you're an authorised centre)

**More course bookings = more authorised centres = more product distribution.**

### 5. Find Authorised Centre 🗺️
Interactive map with filters:
- Motorcycle / Car / MTB
- Öhlins dealer / Service centre / Both
- Ratings and reviews
- Book appointment directly

**Critical for US and Eastern European expansion.** New customers in Ohio or Bulgaria can find their nearest Andreani centre instantly.

### 6. B2B Ordering Portal 🛒
Authorised centres order stock through the app:
- Öhlins products
- Andreani cartridge kits and valving
- Mivv exhausts, Samco hoses, PFC brakes
- Tools: dynos, vacuum pumps, special keys
- Spare parts for customer jobs

**Currently:** Phone call to Pesaro, email order, wait for confirmation.
**With app:** Browse catalogue, see stock availability, order, track delivery.

### 7. Racing Service Portal 🏁
Race teams book Andreani trackside service:
- View race calendar (WSBK, BSB, MX, Enduro)
- Request technician for specific event
- Specify vehicles and suspension
- Pre-event briefing through app
- Live updates during race weekend

**Professional and auditable.** No more last-minute phone calls.

### 8. Digital Service Book 📄
Every service stamped digitally by the authorised centre:
- Fork rebuild ✅ 03 Apr 2026 — TW Suspension Tech, UK
- Rear shock oil change ✅ 15 Jan 2026 — Andreani HQ, Pesaro
- Spring rate change (95 → 100 N/mm) ✅ 28 Nov 2025

**Proof of genuine Andreani/Öhlins service.** Adds resale value. Exportable PDF.

Andreani would be FIRST. Globally.
