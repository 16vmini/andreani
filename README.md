# Andreani Group — Mobile App Concept

A mobile app concept for [Andreani Group](https://www.andreanigroup.com/en/), the Italian suspension specialist — Öhlins exclusive distributor, racing service provider, training academy, and professional tooling manufacturer. Founded by ex-World Motocross champion Giuseppe Andreani in 1987. Operating across motorcycles, cars, and mountain bikes in 14+ countries with a US branch (Andreani USA Inc).

## 🏍️ What Is This?

A premium companion app that connects Andreani's entire ecosystem — riders, drivers, authorized centres, racing teams, and the Andreani HQ — into one digital platform. Product catalogue, suspension setup, dyno reports, service booking, course registration, and dealer network. The first suspension company in the world to offer this.

## 📱 Key Features

### 🏠 My Garage
Register your vehicles and suspension components. Track service intervals, view setup history, get maintenance reminders. "Your Öhlins NIX30 is due for service in 800km." One tap to book at your nearest authorised centre.

### 📊 Digital Dyno Reports
Andreani's DB4 and DS1 dynos generate suspension data. Currently this is probably printed or emailed. The app delivers interactive dyno reports: force/velocity curves, compression/rebound analysis, before/after comparisons. Riders share their data, compare to factory settings, and track improvements over time.

### 🔧 Setup Wizard
Input your bike, weight, riding style, and experience level. The app recommends suspension settings based on Andreani's 35+ years of racing data from thousands of track sessions. Adjustable sliders show preload, compression, rebound. Save setups, compare to pro settings, export for your mechanic.

### 📚 Course Booking
Andreani runs specialist suspension courses for riders and technicians. Browse available courses (motorcycle, car, MTB), view dates and locations, book and pay in-app. Course completion = pathway to becoming an Authorised Centre.

### 🗺️ Find Authorised Centre
Map-based dealer/centre finder. Filter by: motorcycle, car, MTB, Öhlins, service, sales. See ratings, opening hours, book directly. Critical for international expansion.

### 🛒 Product Catalogue & Ordering
Full Öhlins range plus Andreani's own cartridge kits, tools (vacuum pumps, dynos, spring disassemblers), and distributed brands (Mivv, Samco, PFC, BCA). Authorised centres can order stock directly through the app.

### 🏁 Racing Service Portal
Race teams book trackside support. View the race calendar, request a technician, specify vehicle/suspension, track service status during race weekends. Live updates from the Andreani racing truck.

### 🔔 Push Notifications
- "Your service is due — book now at your nearest centre"
- "New Öhlins TTX range now available"
- "Andreani Suspension Course — Pesaro, May 15"
- "Race service available at Misano WSBK round"
- Service completion: "Your fork rebuild is complete — collect tomorrow"

### 📄 Digital Service Book
Complete service history for every suspension component. Stamped digitally by authorised centres. Proof of genuine Andreani/Öhlins service for resale value. Exportable PDF.

## 📱 Tech Stack

- **Framework:** Flutter (Dart)
- **Platforms:** iOS + Android from single codebase
- **Backend:** Firebase / custom API
- **Data:** Dyno data integration (DB4/DS1 export → app)
- **Maps:** Google Maps for dealer finder
- **Payments:** Stripe for courses and product ordering
- **Notifications:** Firebase Cloud Messaging
- **Localisation:** Italian, English, Spanish, German (matches their markets)
